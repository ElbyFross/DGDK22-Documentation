enum Doloro.AI.DAIDebugger.PatchingTool.State
Enumerator
NotStarted 

Patching never started.

Succeed 

Successfully patched.

PartialySuccesed 

APatchLogic.OnPatch(Core.PipelineMap, PatchTicket) executed successfully but the APatchLogic.OnPostPatch(Core.PipelineMap, PatchTicket) was failed.

That can be considered as uncritical issue due to the fact that the APatchLogic.OnPostPatch(Core.PipelineMap, PatchTicket) should be used for finalizing of environment but not the patching the object itself.

Failed 

Patching failed.

InProgress 

Patching started but still in processing.
